Why Reactive UIs?

Reactive UIs are helpful for:

  • Content creation/management (gmail, trello)
  • Live data feeds (facebook, pinterest, instagram)

If you're publishing content:

  • company websites
  • marketing campaigns
  • blogs

...you probably don't need something like React.
